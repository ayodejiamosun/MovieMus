About Us!


MovieMus is a web application that brings together two of your favorite pastimes and/or hobbies: movies and music! With a click of a button, you can see the details of your favorite movies, or movies you would like to see, along with related soundtracks! Now you can experience a movie through its music, to give you a better feel of it! Our front page is filled with trending movies and albums of today, which makes finding your next new favorite movie that much easier! We hope you enjoy your experience! Let us know what new movies or soundtracks you find!
